A year in tea

A year-long, seasonal tea experience — a special tea each month, paired with whole foods and met with unhurried attention, changing with each moment, participant, and season.

What it will hold

Monthly features

Each session highlights a special tea, paired with seasonal Pacific Northwest foods.

Progressive journey

Beginning with familiar teas and gradually exploring more complex traditions.

Embodied studies

Sessions as sensory studies in awareness, reflection, and shared attention.

Emotion & change

Each gathering unique — a shifting atmosphere with moment, participant, and season.

Monthly learning focus

Noticing flavour, experimenting with brewing, reflecting on origin and ritual.

Starter kit

A tea-tasting journal and a guide for personal reflection.

Beginner's glossary

Pronunciation and cultural context for foundational tea terms.

Resources & prompts

Support for both individual and group engagement.

Planned form. An interactive seasonal map of the year — twelve months, twelve teas — showing what is in season and in focus, so the experience can be followed month by month as it unfolds.
